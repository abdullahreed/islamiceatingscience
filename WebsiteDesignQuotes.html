God-Centred Design Quote

بِسْمِ اللهِ الرَّحْمٰنِ الرَّحِيمِ

الَّذِى خَلَقَ سَبْعَ سَمَٰوَاتٍ طِبَاقًا مَا تَرَىٰ فِي خَلْقِ الرَّحْمَٰنِ مِن تَفَوُتٌ فَارْجِعِ الْبَصَرَ هَلْ تَرَىٰ مِن فُطُورِ

He who created the seven heavens in layers. You do not see any inconsistency in the creation of the Most Merciful. So return your vision to the sky: do you see any flaws?

The Dominion, Verse 3: The surah builds faith by describing God's sovereignty with the most compelling description. It includes striking questions that leave the reader with no other option but a humble acknowledgement of his perfection in the construction of the universe.
